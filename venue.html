Our Venue

Kouros is a beautiful hotel situated on a cliff top in Mykonos town, Greece. We instantly fell in love with its panoramic views of the town, the sea, and the breathtaking sunsets. We have since found ourselves back here twice, making some life long friends along the way. We knew this was the perfect place to bring our family and friends to celebrate a new chapter in our lives!
